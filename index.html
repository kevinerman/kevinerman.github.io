About Me

Hi there! My name is Kevin Erman and on behalf of my serious, unsmiling face, I'd like to thank you for stopping by to check out my portfolio! I'm a new website developer with an Associate's Degree in Performance Theater for some reason. I grew up in Dayton, Ohio, but five years ago I decided to trade chilli dogs for deep dish pizza and move out to Chicago. Okay actually I still eat plenty of chilli dogs but now I eat deep dish pizza too. Variety!

Over the years I've built up an extensive background in customer service with over 10 years of retail experience. I learned with first hand experience how to deal with a wide variety of customer issues both as an associate and as a manager. My retail experience also helped me to learn many songs by heart, often against my will, simply by hearing them so many times. No bonus points for guessing why I left retail!

I spend my free time losing to 12 year olds in Fortnite, watching superhero shows on TV, and performing improv comedy. One day I would like to leverage all of the time I've spent playing video games into creating them. Until that time comes I would be satisfied with one Battle Royale win in Fortnite. Seriously why are these 12 year olds so good at this game?
